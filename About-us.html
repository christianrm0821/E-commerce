About US

Welcome to Walking On Earth, your go-to destination for stylish and affordable clothing right from the heart of Hayward, California! Founded with a passion for fashion and a commitment to community, we're more than just a clothing reseller — we're a local business dedicated to providing trendy apparel while fostering connections within our neighborhood.

Our Story: Walking On Earth started as a humble venture out of a garage in Hayward. What began as a passion project quickly grew into something more as we saw the opportunity to offer quality clothing options to our local community. From those early days, we've remained true to our roots, embracing the spirit of entrepreneurship and the unique character of our city.

Our Mission: At Walking on Earth, our mission is simple: to offer stylish clothing at affordable prices while providing exceptional customer service. We believe that everyone deserves to look and feel their best without breaking the bank. By curating a diverse selection of on-trend pieces, we strive to empower our customers to express themselves through their personal style.

What Sets Us Apart:
Local Focus:Local Focus: We're proud to call Hayward home, and we're dedicated to supporting our local community. When you shop with us, you're not just buying clothes — you're investing in the growth and vibrancy of our city.
Quality Selection: From everyday essentials to statement pieces, we carefully select each item in our collection to ensure quality, style, and affordability. Whether you're searching for the perfect outfit or a unique accessory, you'll find it here.
Service: As a small business, we value each and every customer. That's why we're committed to providing personalized service and a seamless shopping experience. Have a question or need assistance? Our friendly team is here to help. Get in Touch We love hearing from our customers! Whether you have a question, feedback, or just want to say hello, we're here for you. Feel free to reach out to us via email, phone, or social media — we'd love to connect with you!
Thank you for supporting walking on Earth. Together, let's redefine the way you shop for clothes.
Happy shopping!
Jairo Martinez, Walking on Earth
